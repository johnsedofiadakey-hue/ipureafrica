How iPure Works

  • 01

    Pre-Filter Stage

    Captures large particles — pet hair, dust mites, lint — before reaching the core system.

  • 02

    HEPA H13 Core Filtration

    Removes 99.97% of particles ≥0.3 microns including PM2.5, pollen, mold, and bacteria.

  • 03

    Activated Carbon Layer

    Eliminates VOCs, smoke, formaldehyde, and odors through dense carbon mesh absorption.

  • 04

    UV-C Sterilization (PureMax Pro)

    Ultraviolet light destroys viruses and bacteria at DNA level — hospital-grade sterilization.

  • 05

    Smart Monitoring

    Real-time AQI sensors auto-adjust fan speed. Full control via iPure Smart App.

Company Description

Who We Are

iPure Air Purifier is a modern air purification brand designed specifically for African environments, starting with Ghana. Our product line is manufactured through a trusted OEM within the Xiaomi ecosystem, ensuring premium quality and smart functionality — without being sold under the Xiaomi name.

Developed by iPure Group of Companies, iPure is positioned as a homegrown African brand delivering cutting-edge, affordable air purification to highly populated indoor spaces including hospitals, corporate offices, hotels, banks, schools, government offices, and religious centers.

According to the WHO, indoor air pollution accounts for a significant percentage of respiratory diseases in sub-Saharan Africa. Urbanization and construction in cities like Accra are increasing airborne contaminants daily. We are here to change that.

Setup

Installation Guide

Get your iPure unit up and running in minutes. No tools required for most installations.

01
📦

Unbox & Inspect

Remove your iPure unit from packaging. Check all accessories: power cable, pre-filter, instruction manual, and warranty card are included.

02
📍

Choose Your Location

Place your unit centrally in the room, at least 20cm from walls. Avoid corners and areas behind furniture. Elevated placement improves circulation.

03
🔌

Power On

Connect to power. The unit performs a self-diagnostic on first start. The AQI display will show current air quality within 30 seconds of startup.

04
📱

Connect Smart App

Download the iPure Smart App. Tap "Add Device", scan the QR code on your unit, and connect to your Wi-Fi. Full control is available within 2 minutes.

iPure Warranty

Every iPure product is built to perform, and backed by our comprehensive warranty program.

🛡️

5-Year Unit Warranty

All iPure purifiers are covered for 5 years against manufacturing defects. Free repair or replacement guaranteed within this period for qualifying faults.

🔧

1-Year Parts Warranty

All internal components including fans, PCBs, sensors, and UV-C lamps are covered for 12 months. Free part replacement at any authorized iPure service center.

🚀

48-Hour Service Response

For institutional and corporate clients, we guarantee a technical response within 48 hours of any warranty claim or service request. Same-day for critical environments.

Warranty Terms & Coverage

Warranty covers all defects in materials and workmanship under normal use and service conditions.

Free replacement filters are not included in the hardware warranty but are available via our Maintenance Subscription plan.

Warranty is voided by unauthorized modifications, use of non-iPure replacement filters, or damage caused by improper installation or power surges.

Accidental damage and liquid ingress are not covered. Extended accidental damage protection is available as an add-on for institutional clients.

Warranty claims can be filed by contacting our support team with your proof of purchase and unit serial number. Claims are processed within 5 business days.

Corporate clients with maintenance subscriptions receive priority warranty processing and dedicated case management.
